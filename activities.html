Kazakhstan Activities
Hiking in the Tian Shan Mountains

Explore breathtaking trails in Almaty’s surrounding mountains, including hikes to Big Almaty Lake and Medeu.

Skiing at Shymbulak Ski Resort

One of the ski resorts in Central Asia, offering stunning slopes and modern facilities.

Attending the Nauryz Festival

Celebrated in March, this traditional festival includes music, dance, and Kazakh cuisine.

Camping in the Kazakh Steppe

Experience the vast open landscapes and unique wildlife of Kazakhstan’s steppe regions.

Exploring the Kolsai Lakes

Known as the "Pearls of the Northern Tien Shan," these stunning mountain lakes are perfect for boating and hiking.

Exploring Tamgaly Petroglyphs

A UNESCO-listed site featuring over 5,000 ancient rock carvings dating back thousands of years.

Touring the Baikonur Cosmodrome

The world's first and largest space launch facility, where visitors can see rocket launches if timed right.

Shopping at Green Bazaar in Almaty

A great place to experience local flavors, buy dried fruits, spices, and traditional Kazakh products.
