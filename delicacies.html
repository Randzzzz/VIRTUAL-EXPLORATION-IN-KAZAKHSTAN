Kazakhstan Deliacies
Kurt

Hard, salty dried cheese balls made from fermented milk.

Shubat

Fermented camel milk, known for its health benefits.

Baursak

Deep-fried dough balls, often served as a snack or with tea.

Shelpek

A flat, fried bread similar to naan.

Beshbarmak

The national dish, consisting of boiled meat (usually lamb or beef) served over wide noodles with onion broth.

Sorpa

A rich meat broth, typically served with beshbarmak or as a standalone soup.

Manti

Steamed dumplings filled with minced meat, often served with sour cream.

Lagman

A noodle dish with beef, vegetables, and a rich, spiced broth, influenced by Uyghur cuisine.
