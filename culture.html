Kazakhstan Culture
Kazakh Yurt

The "Kazakh Yurt"is a traditional portable home used by nomads. Made of wooden frames and felt coverings, it provides shelter from harsh weather. Today, yurts symbolize Kazakh heritage and are used during cultural events and festivals.

Kazakh Horse Games

Horses play a vital role in Kazakh culture. Traditional horse games like Kokpar (a sport similar to polo), Baiga (long-distance horse racing), and Kyz Kuu (a playful chase game between men and women on horseback) showcase the deep connection between Kazakhs and their horses.

Kazakh Handicrafts

Kazakhstan's handicrafts reflect its nomadic heritage, with intricate felt rugs (syrmak), embroidered textiles (tuskiiz), and hand-carved wooden yurt structures. Skilled artisans also create silver jewelry, leather horse tack, and traditional musical instruments like the dombra.
